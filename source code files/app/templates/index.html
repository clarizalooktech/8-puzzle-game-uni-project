8 Puzzle

The classic 8-puzzle game is a sliding puzzle invented and popularized by Noyes Palmer,It is played on a 3-by-3 grid with 8 square blocks labeled 1 through 8 and a blank square. One cell of the frame is always empty thus making it possible to move an adjacent numbered tile into the empty cell. Blocks in the same row or column of the open position can be moved by sliding them horizontally or vertically, respectively.

The rule of the puzzle is to move the numbered blocks around until they are in numerical order.

The time you spend to solve the puzzle will be recorded. Start play today and Share the result with your friend!
