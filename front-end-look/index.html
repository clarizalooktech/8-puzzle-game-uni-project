Tile Swapping Game

This lovely puzzle game will definitely become one of the favorites of the kids. The object of the game is to swap the positions of the tiles so that the correct numbering will be in place.

When the game starts, the numbers are swapped in different boxes. Click any of the pieces to select it, then click an adjacent tile to swap the positions of the two pieces. Continue the process until the numbers are in the correct series.

The number of moves you have made and the amount of time you have spent will be counted on the two sides of the picture. Be the first one to accomplish the task among family and friends!
